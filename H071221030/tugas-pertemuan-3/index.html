WELCOME TO
ASSASSIN'S CREED UNIVERSE

Play your way through history in the award-winning video game series. Assassin's Creed immerses you in the memories of your ancestors, fighting to protect free will at pivotal moments in human history.

Assassin's Creed (2007)

Assassin's Creed 1 is an action-adventure game where players step into the shoes of Desmond Miles, reliving the memories of his ancestor Alta√Ør Ibn-La'Ahad as he navigates the intricate world of assassins and Templars during the Third Crusade in the Holy Land.

Assassin's Creed II (2009)

Assassin's Creed II, released in 2009, follows the journey of Ezio Auditore da Firenze, an Italian nobleman turned assassin, as he seeks revenge and uncovers a conspiracy in Renaissance-era Italy, offering a rich blend of historical exploration and stealthy action gameplay.

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood (2010)

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood, released in 2010, continues the story of Ezio Auditore da Firenze as he establishes a brotherhood of assassins to combat the Templar threat in Rome, offering a seamless open-world experience, strategic management of assassin recruits, and a compelling narrative in the Assassin's Creed series.

Assassin's Creed Revelations (2011)

Assassin's Creed Revelations, released in 2011, concludes the Ezio Auditore trilogy as he travels to Constantinople, unraveling the secrets of Altair and Desmond's predicament, while introducing new gameplay elements and exploring the aging assassin's legacy in the series.

Assassin's Creed III (2012)

Assassin's Creed III, released in 2012, is set during the American Revolution and follows the journey of Connor Kenway, a Native American assassin, as he fights for justice and freedom amidst the backdrop of historical events like the Boston Tea Party and the Battle of Bunker Hill, while also exploring the ongoing conflict between assassins and Templars in colonial America.

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag (2013)

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag, released in 2013, shifts the series to the Golden Age of Piracy, where players assume the role of Edward Kenway, a pirate and assassin, as he explores the Caribbean, engages in naval battles, and becomes embroiled in the age-old conflict between assassins and Templars, all set against the backdrop of the high seas and lush tropical islands.

Assassin's Creed Rogue (2014)

Assassin's Creed Rogue, released in 2014, offers a unique perspective as players take on the role of Shay Patrick Cormac, a former assassin turned Templar, as he hunts down his former comrades and uncovers the Templar side of the conflict during the Seven Years' War, providing a fresh narrative twist in the Assassin's Creed series.

Assassin's Creed Unity (2014)

Assassin's Creed Unity, released in 2014, transports players to the vibrant and tumultuous setting of Paris during the French Revolution, featuring Arno Dorian as the protagonist. The game focuses on his quest for redemption and justice within the backdrop of a city in turmoil, offering enhanced parkour mechanics and cooperative multiplayer modes.

Assassin's Creed Syndicate (2015)

Assassin's Creed Syndicate, released in 2015, is set in Victorian-era London and features twin protagonists, Jacob and Evie Frye, as they lead the charge against the Templar-controlled organized crime in the city. The game showcases a dynamic and evolving industrial London, complete with historical figures and introduces new gameplay mechanics like grappling hooks and horse-drawn carriages.

Assassin's Creed Origins (2017)

Assassin's Creed Origins, released in 2017, transports players to ancient Egypt, allowing them to step into the sandals of Bayek of Siwa, a Medjay and one of the earliest assassins. The game delves into the origins of the Brotherhood and offers a vast open world with RPG elements, intricate combat, and a rich historical setting to explore.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey (2018)

Assassin's Creed Odyssey, released in 2018, takes players to ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War, where they assume the role of either Alexios or Kassandra, descendants of the legendary Spartan king Leonidas. The game offers a vast open world, RPG elements, and the freedom to shape the story through choices, as players embark on a journey of epic proportions in the world of ancient Greece.

Assassin's Creed Valhalla (2020)

Assassin's Creed Valhalla, released in 2020, immerses players in the Viking Age as they take on the role of Eivor, a Norse warrior who leads their clan from Norway to England in search of a new home. The game blends Viking history and mythology, offering a vast open world, settlement management, and a compelling narrative that explores the clash of cultures during this turbulent time in history.
