CAT EYE NAILS

Cat eye nails refers to a nail design style that creates an effect resembling the shimmering eyes of a cat. This style is typically achieved by using nail polish or gel with a light-reflecting effect that resembles the glistening eyes of a cat. It provides a mysterious and elegant look to the nails with striking light play.

SUMMER NAILS

Summer nails" refers to nail designs specifically crafted for the summer season, featuring vibrant colors, nature-inspired motifs like flowers and suns, and spirited touches to reflect the positive energy and cheerfulness of summer.



SIMPLE GEL NAILS

Simpel nails" refers to a nail design that is simple and minimalistic in nature. This style often uses neutral colors or uncomplicated patterns, creating an elegant and clean look for the nails. Simpel nails emphasize the beauty of simplicity and timeless elegance in nail design.


CUTE NAILS

Cute nails" refers to nail designs that are adorable and charming. This style often involves the use of bright colors, cute patterns like pets, small flowers, or endearing motifs such as hearts or stars. Cute nails add an element of playfulness and delightful appeal to the nail appearance.
